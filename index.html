BOOK A VISIT

If you’d like to learn about joining our school, we’d love to invite you to a personalised visit where you can get to know us, and we can learn about your child and their needs to enable them to thrive at our school. Our meetings will take place on weekdays between 09:00 and 16:00. If these are not convenient for you then please do let us know and we will endeavour to accommodate. To book a tour please complete the enquiry form and share your request including date and timings. Our Admissions team will contact you with possible time slots. Each meeting will last approximately one hour and will be led by one of our Academic Leadership team.

WHO WE ARE


We believe in focusing on all aspects of the person with a view to equipping each boy to be able to face the real world boldly and be truly successful in life. We believe that leadership in men is the backbone of all societies. Dawamu offers a unique experience which produces confident, well rounded individuals. We are specialist boys only, Kenyan curriculum boarding school with a holistic approach to education.
